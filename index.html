About Me

Hello! I am Divine Ogbodo, a student in the department of genetics and biotechnology. I am passionate about technology and machine learning. I enjoy exploring the intersections of biology and technology, and I am particularly interested in how machine learning can be applied to genetic research.

Blog

Gene Sequencing: The Future of Medicine

Gene sequencing is revolutionizing the field of medicine. By understanding the precise order of nucleotides in DNA, scientists can identify genetic disorders, predict disease risk, and develop personalized treatments. In this blog, I will delve into the latest advancements in gene sequencing technologies and their implications for healthcare.

Gene Modification: Ethical and Technological Perspectives

Gene modification holds the potential to eradicate genetic diseases and enhance human capabilities. However, it also raises ethical concerns about the extent to which we should alter our genetic makeup. This blog will explore both the scientific breakthroughs and the ethical debates surrounding gene editing technologies such as CRISPR.
